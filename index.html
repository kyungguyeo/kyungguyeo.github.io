Research

This is quickly becoming a big area of research in the health world, and many big organizations are aligning themselves to try to pour in more of their efforts towards SDOH.

For example, HealthyPeople is a large-scale decade-long effort to track national progress against specific health goals, and a focus on SDOH is now one of the new objectives for 2020.

The Problem

It's hard to identify at-risk "social" patients the traditional way

While it is good that we have all of these groups recognizing the concerns surround SDOH, there haven’t been that much done to help address the issues today. There are many today who suffer from this condition, and suffer silently, not knowing that they can get treatment, or not knowing even that they should be getting preventive treatment for their situation.
